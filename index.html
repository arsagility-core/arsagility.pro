What is Arsa

Arsa is a cryptocurrency that aims to revolutionize the community by rewarding the community through a unique 70% "Proof of Stake" (PoS) and 20% "Proof of Works" (PoW) mechanism.

Arsa aims to create a sustainable and versatile community ecosystem and revolutionize the traditional trading industry towards digital by providing a platform that rewards communities for their participation and involvement in various transactions, while enabling the creation, trading and utilization of digital assets.
Arsa has the potential to create a sustainable and versatile ecosystem that is profitable for businesses, communities, investors and developers.

Why Choose Arsagility?

With the Go Beyond Next spirit that demonstrates creativity we will act differently and always go beyond the reach in innovation.

Secure

Arsagility is based on the principles of cryptography, decentralization and consensus which ensures trustworthy transactions.

Private

PrivateSend is a trustless method of running a sequence of transactions such that an observer has difficulty tracing transaction history.

Cpu Mining

How can I get ARGY coins?
ARGY can be obtained by process of mining, running a smartnode or participating in community servers. Helping the Arsagility ecosystem by developing or adopting to existing apps is also rewarded.
How can I mine Arsagility coins?
In order to mine check miningpoolstats and pick a mining pool (consider keeping pool's under 51% overall hashrate for helping network stabilization). If you need more help check our #mining discord channel.

What is a smartnode?
Smartnodes are servers designed to provide advanced services and governance on the blockchain. Smartnodes host full copies of the blockchain and provide a unique second layer of services to the network, facilitating advanced functions such as InstantSend and PrivateSend. Smartnodes must be backed by collateral, and in return their operators receive regular payment for the services they provide to the network.
How can I run a Smartnodes?
In order to start a Smartnodes you need to have 900,000 ARGY as collateral and a server where to run the software. There are two options available : Hosted Smartnodes or Self-operated Smartnodes. Both install methods are explained in the Smartnodes setup guide.
What other benefits are from owning a Smartnodes?
Smartnodes operators are given 70% of the block reward to fund community projects supporting the Arsagility ecosystem.
Is it a secure ?
Absolutely not. Arsagility is an experimental cryptocurrency that it's not regulated or backed up by a company. You can send Arsagility to other people, receive some or exchange it at the markets.
